## Slide 3: Why Crypto & Multi-Factor Strategy?

**Market: BTC/USDT**
- 24/7 continuous trading, no open/close constraints → ideal for intraday strategies
- Deep order book, high liquidity
- Fully public data, free API access

**Strategy: Multi-Factor Momentum**
- Single indicators (e.g. moving averages) are prone to false signals
- Multi-factor combination cross-validates across dimensions, reducing false positives
- Momentum effects in crypto markets are well-documented in academic literature

> One-liner: we don't guess — we let the data speak

## Slide 4: Strategy Core — Three Factors

**Core idea: composite score → act only when the threshold is crossed**

| Factor | Weight | Calculation | When It Scores High |
|--------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Price Momentum | 40% | 20-minute return | Persistent up move adds score; down move subtracts |
| Volume | 30% | Current vol / 20-bar average vol | Volume-price alignment doubles the score |
| Volatility | 30% | Price standard deviation | Calm market adds score; violent swings down-weight |

**Trigger Rules**
- Composite score > +0.4 → **Buy** (deploy 95% of capital)
- Composite score < −0.4 → **Sell** (liquidate)
- Otherwise → **Hold**

> Parameters optimised via grid search (36 combinations); Sharpe ratio selected as the objective

## Slide 9: TCA — Transaction Cost Analysis

**What is TCA?**
Quantifying every trade's "hidden costs" — the real expenses incurred

| Cost Type | Amount (This Backtest) | Notes |
|-----------|----------------------|-------|
| Commission | ~50 USDT | Binance charges 0.1% of trade value |
| Estimated Slippage | ~25 USDT | Extra cost from price movement at order fill |
| Total Cost | ~75 USDT | ~6% of total profit |

**Conclusion:** Cost-to-profit ratio ~6%, within acceptable range (industry standard < 10%)

## Slide 10: Risk Control Framework

**Three-Layer Protection:**

```
Layer 1: Position Sizing
   Each buy uses at most 95% of available cash — always keep liquidity reserve

Layer 2: Max Drawdown Stop
   Account drops ≥ 15% from peak → all trading automatically halted

Layer 3: Strict Signal Filtering
   All three factors must agree; threshold ±0.4 (grid search optimum)
   Any factor anomaly → stay flat (Hold)
```
